Kazi Nazrul Islam

The national poet of Bangladesh

Rebel Post

Kazi Nazrul Islam is the national poet of Bangladesh who is escpecially recognized as Bidrohi Kobi(rebel poet) around the country. Nazrul is a famous poet, musician and philospher of Bangladesh. The fanous poet Nazrul's writings deal with the revolutionary attempt helped him to be a national poet of Bangladesh. He has raised his voice against any kinds of facism and oppression and got popularity swiftly around the Indian Subcontinent.

Awesome Writer

Nazrul's writings explored themes such as love, freedom, humanity, and revolution. He opposed all forms of bigotry and fundamentalism, includiing religious, caste-based and gender-based. Throughouthis career, Nazrul wrote short stories, novels, and essays but is best known for his songs and poems. He pioneered new music forms such as Bengali ghazals. Nazrul wrote and composed music for nearly 4,000 songs(many recorded on HMV and gramophone records), collectively known as Nazrul Geeti.

"Love has no meaning or amount."-Kazi Nazrul Islam
